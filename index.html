Privacy Policy

Effective date: 4/10/2025

This Baby Monitor app connects to a Raspberry Pi device on your local network to provide live audio and video monitoring. All communication happens entirely within your local area network (LAN) and is never transmitted to any external servers.

Information Collection and Use

The app does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All video and audio streams remain local to your devices and are not uploaded or shared over the internet.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking tools.

Security

Because all data transmission occurs within your local network, the security of your data depends on the security of your Wi-Fi network and Raspberry Pi configuration. We recommend using strong passwords and keeping your devices up to date.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page.
