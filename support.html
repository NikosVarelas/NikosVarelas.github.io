About Baby Monitor App

The Baby Monitor App is designed to provide parents with peace of mind by allowing them to monitor their baby using a Raspberry Pi device. The app connects to your Raspberry Pi over your local Wi-Fi network to stream live audio and video, helping you keep an eye on your little one in real time.

Unlike cloud-based baby monitors, this app prioritizes your privacy — all communication happens entirely within your local network (LAN), meaning your data never leaves your home.

Key Features

  • Local-only live audio and video streaming
  • Noise level graph with customizable alerts
  • Lightweight design and simple setup
  • No internet connection or third-party servers required

Our Mission

Our goal is to create a safe, private, and user-friendly monitoring experience for families who value control and data security.
